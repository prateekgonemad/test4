Our Apps Features

Location Filter

OffSlate makes extensive use of location as a key filtering tool. It aids in the search for a job that is convenient for you. Enhances your work-life balance.

AI-Matching

Simply by searching the skill name as per the location range you like, OffSlate will connect you directly to the service provider according to the service you require. It will come in handy in any form of situation. You will be able to post a service based on your skill, and the client will arrange an appointment based on that skill requirement.

Internet Calling

Users can contact to service provider through our internet calling service.

IN-APP CV

Insert the necessary information and the AI will generate a CV in pdf format, which will be uploaded. It can also be downloaded if desired.
